Stevenson WYSE Invitational

May 28, 2016. 8a - 12p.
Stevenson West Building.

Why?

We love science. That's why. And now we're calling out to all middle schoolers in order to share our love for science. Join us for a morning of fun, and compete with your peers from other middle schools in various events.

How does it work?

Teams of up to 7 will participate in three events: multiple choice academic test, a research paper contest, and finally, a hands-on building challenge! All of these events will test not only your knowledge of science and engineering concepts, but also how well you can put them into practice.

The multiple choice round will consist of two back-to-back tests: you choose which tests to take! Like in the University of Illinois' WYSE contest, the seven subjects you can compete in are: biology, chemistry, computer science, engineering graphics, english, math, and physics. Top scoring teams and individuals will be recognized at the awards ceremony.
